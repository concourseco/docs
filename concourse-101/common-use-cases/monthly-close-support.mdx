### 

Turn your monthly close from a scramble into a repeatable workflow.

With Concourse you can:

- Compare actuals vs budget and prior periods in seconds
- Drill into drivers by segment, product, region, or channel
- Generate draft variance commentary that is ready for review

Example prompts:

- "Walk me through the biggest drivers of variance in net revenue vs budget this month."
- "Summarize changes in operating expenses by department and flag anything unusual."
